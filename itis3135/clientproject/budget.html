    Identify All Income Sources

    To accurately define all your income sources, begin by reviewing your regular and irregular earnings. This includes full-time or part-time wages, freelance work, side gigs, government benefits, child support, rental income, and any other incoming funds. Refer to the green income table in the spreadsheet below as a guide—it breaks down typical categories and helps you avoid missing smaller, less obvious income streams. Being thorough here ensures your budget reflects your true financial situation.
