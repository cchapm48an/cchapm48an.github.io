Steam

Steam is a digital distribution platform for video games, offering game purchases, community features, and software.

  • Contrast: Well-executed dark theme with bright highlights for readability and focus.
  • Repetition: Consistent use of fonts, colors, and interface elements throughout the site.
  • Alignment: Well-structured layout with properly aligned sections and content.
  • Proximity: Logical grouping of related elements, such as game details and purchase options.

Wish

Wish is an online marketplace that offers a wide range of low-cost items, primarily sourced from international sellers.

  • Contrast: Low contrast between background colors and shopping elements makes it difficult to distinguish sections, with bright visuals straining the eyes.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent font styling and varying text sizes make some content hard to read and visually unappealing..
  • Alignment: Product listings are aligned, but the cluttered layout makes it difficult to focus on individual items.
  • Proximity: Overloaded interface with too many items closely packed together, making navigation difficult and overwhelming to browse.
